Sub-zero Bio

Subzero, as known as Kuai Lang, was born on May 1, 1970. He is one of the prestigious members of the Lin Kuei clan. The Lin Clan are a group of assassins where Kuai Lang and his brother Bi-Han. During the first Mortal Kombat tournament Bi-Han, firstly known as “Subzero” was killed by rival clans member Hanzo Hasashi, Scorpion, of the Shirai Ryu clan. From death of this brother Kuai lang rose as Sub-Zero in honor of his brother. He swore vengeance against Scorpion and entered into Mortal Kombat in search for Scorpion. This moment was the beginning of an eternal feud between the two assassins.
