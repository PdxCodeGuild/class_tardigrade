Elon Musk

Elon Musk is an an entrepreneur and business magnate. He is the founder, CEO and Chief Engineer at SpaceX; early-stage investor, CEO and Product Architect of Tesla; founder of The Boring Company, and co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI. With an estimated net worth of around $255 billion as of December 2021, Elon Musk is the wealthiest person in the world.
