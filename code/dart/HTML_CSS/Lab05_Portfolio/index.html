About Me

I'm a graduate of PDX Code Guild's Full-Stack Software Development Bootcamp.

I'm a Recent graduate of PDX Code Guild's Full-Stack Software Development Bootcamp, where I learned Python, JavaScript, Django, HTML/CSS.

Additionally, I served eight (8) years in the US Air Force, stationed in Albuquerque, NM as well as Fussa, Japan. During that time, I earned a Bachelor’s in Aeronautics from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University and an Associate's in Criminal Justice from the Community College of the Air Force (CCAF). My four (4)+ years as a supervisor provided me with excellent leadership experience by leading/supervising 20+ employees on a daily basis. I was also able to learn valuable skills such as effective communication, working as a team player, and fostering a collaborative and safe work environment.
