Azores Islands

The Azore islands are part of Portugal and Europe's best kept secret. They are located 870 miles west of lisbon and located in the Atlantic ocean. Despite its quite northern location, the Azore islands have mild temperatures year round thanks to the Gulf stream which keeps temperatures around 61 and 77 °F.

    The Azores Islands consist of the following:
  1. São Miguel
  2. Santa Maria,
  3. the Formigas islets;
      central group:
    • Faial
    • Pico
    • São Jorge
    • Terceira
    • and Graciosa
      northwestern group:
    • Flores
    • Corvo

Ironically, the Azores are considered the next Iceland thanks to increasing numbers of tourists.-Conde Nast
Fun fact: Canadian singer Nelly Furtado's parents are from the Azores Islands.
